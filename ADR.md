--------------------------------------------------
Summary
--------------------------------------------------

The general structure is an essential piece in the development of the webpage. The way that the foundation is laid out will determine the rest of the the structure. The basic django app structure chosen reflects the skills and experience of the development team and the overall function of the webpage to be created.

--------------------------------------------------
Drivers
--------------------------------------------------

The beginning of the project is the most influential stage as it determines how the rest of the project will look. A lengthy discussion was held as to determine the specific solutions that would shape the structure of the rest of the project. We needed a solution that was modular to adapt to the possibly changing features of the platform that matched the skills of the group members.

--------------------------------------------------
Options
--------------------------------------------------

Django Project Structure
    A project made using a django project structure would be highly modular and customisable. Prebuilt modules would save time during development and make the overall development easier in both workload and management.

--------------------------------------------------
Options Analysis
--------------------------------------------------

The Django Project structure was the only option seriously considered as all members have experience in Django and basic python. The structure is widely documented and will make for a quick development cycle that can adapt to however the project may change direction.

--------------------------------------------------
Recommendation
--------------------------------------------------

The Django project structure was selected as it was the option that best suited the structure of the project and the skills of the individual team members whilst also being easy to split up.

--------------------------------------------------
Confirmation
--------------------------------------------------

The particular parts of development of the would be split upon the particular group members and communicated within the chat channels of the group with regular updates written to declare any changes made during development that can be used elsewhere in the project.
